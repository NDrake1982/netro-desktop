Controllers

Each Netro controller has a 12-character hex serial number. Find it in the Netro mobile app: open the controller → Settings → look for "Serial number".

Plant sensors (Whisperer)

Optional. Each Whisperer has its own serial. Sensors show soil moisture, temperature, light, and battery — and warn you when one goes offline.

Privacy

Your serial numbers are stored only in this browser's local storage. They're sent directly to api.netrohome.com when the dashboard fetches data — never to any third-party server. Use the same page on another device by entering the same serials there.
